Flat Iron Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of flat iron steak
  • 2,5 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon of chopped fresh rosemary and chives
  • 1/4 cup of Cabernet Sauvignon (or other dry red wine)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dry mustard powder
