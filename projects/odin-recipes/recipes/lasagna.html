Lasagna

The world's best lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, fennel seeds, black pepper and Italian seasoning
  • Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare all the necessary ingredients.
  2. Layer the lasagna in the following order
    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top the remaining parmesan.
  3. The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
    Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
    Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
